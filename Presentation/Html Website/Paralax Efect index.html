Instructions

Start is in the menu, here you can click on HowTo to show the game instructions again. Pressing the space bar or clicking Play will take you to the start screen. Pressing one of the game buttons will start the game.

The game

When a mind overcomes it, it loses one of its three lives, and the nearest Pacman must complete the task of clearing the labyrinth of pills and earning as many points as possible from the starting position. After the last life the game is over and the main menu follows.

Pacman - your own character - is selected with the arrow keys, or W / A / S / D emotional. Where he comes along, he eats the yellow pills, each simple pill brings 10, each power pill 50 points. When all the pills are eaten, the level is done and the next one follows.

When you get into one of the two tunnels, Pacman comes out on the other side, and can thus shower the ghosts.

A visit to one of the four spirits costs a lifetime. But when Pacman eats one of the four big dots, the ghosts turn dark blue at short notice. Eat the spirits after eating a power pill, you will get the score

● 200 for the 1. ghost

● 400 for the 2.

● 800 for the 3.

● and 1600 for the last ghost.

By collecting the fruits, which appear after some time, one can either receive extra points, or receive a life back.

The goal

The goal of the game is to collect all the points in each level. Without overcoming one of the ghosts.
